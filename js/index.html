Flights Throughout the Year

Throughout the year the number of people booking flights can vary wildly. As we can see from the plots to the left, the number of flights increase at both the start and end of the year, with a sharp decrease around the middle. While this in turn causes a much larger number of delayed flights, when looking at the actual proportions we can see that the proportion of delays peak in June, the month with the lowest number of flights.
